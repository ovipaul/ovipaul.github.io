About Me

I am a freshman at Stanford University studying Computer Science! I love writing code, particularly in Python and Swift.

I believe in the importance of equal access to computer science education. I love teaching workshops about computer science topics that I've studied. Past workshops I've hosted include Raspberry Pi & Python, Intro to Machine Learning, and Control Structures in Swift. I also write Turkish articles on iOS programming and design principles at TurkishKit.

Last year, I attended WWDC18 as a Scholarship Recipient, and showcased my playground at the Swift Educator Summit in Berlin.

Besides programming, I enjoy working on designs in Adobe Illustrator, tending to my Animal Crossing town, reading any book I can get my hands on, and going to the gym.
